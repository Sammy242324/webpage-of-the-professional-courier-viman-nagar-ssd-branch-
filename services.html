What We Courier

We handle everyday courier needs for both personal and business customers.

  • Documents: certificates, agreements, office files, application forms
  • Small parcels: gifts, clothes, books, accessories, household items
  • Business shipments: samples, invoices, brochures, lightweight office stock
  • E-commerce packets: customer orders, return parcels, replacement items

Packing and Item Guidance

Items should be securely packed and accurately declared at booking. Fragile items need protective inner packing.

For every laptop booking, we pack securely with bubble wrap first and then move forward with safe dispatch processing.

We do not accept prohibited, hazardous, flammable, illegal, or restricted items. Please confirm with the branch team before booking if you are unsure.
