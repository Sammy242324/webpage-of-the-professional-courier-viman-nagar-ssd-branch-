Important Note

Please pack items securely and declare contents correctly at booking.

Restricted, hazardous, flammable, illegal, and prohibited items are not accepted.

Restricted and Prohibited Items

To ensure safe transport and compliance, the following items are not accepted for courier booking:

  • Explosives, fireworks, compressed gas, flammable liquids, chemicals
  • Illegal goods, narcotics, counterfeit products, banned substances
  • Currency, precious metals, high-value jewelry without approved declaration
  • Live animals, biological samples, and unsafe perishable goods
  • Any item restricted by local, state, national, or international regulation

If you are unsure, contact our branch before dispatch to avoid delays.
